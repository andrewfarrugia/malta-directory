Prices are indicative and may vary depending on scope, location, and provider.

Cleaning services in Malta are usually priced by time, property size, and depth of cleaning. Standard recurring visits are lower per session than one-off deep cleans.

## Typical rate guide

| Cleaning type | Typical range |
| --- | --- |
| Standard hourly cleaning | EUR 12-22/hr |
| Deep clean (small flat) | EUR 90-190 |
| Post-renovation clean | EUR 180-450 |
| Office cleaning contract | Custom monthly rate |

## Inclusions to confirm

- Supplies and equipment included or extra.
- Number of cleaners assigned.
- Appliance interior cleaning included or add-on.
- Travel fee for your locality.

## Cost-saving tips

Bundle recurring services, define a checklist before the first session, and avoid scope creep during the visit. A clear checklist keeps results consistent and prevents disputes.
