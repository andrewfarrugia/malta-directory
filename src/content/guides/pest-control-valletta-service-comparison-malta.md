This guide provides an independent checklist for comparing pest control services in Valletta, Malta. Listings and provider details can change over time.

## Local context in Valletta
- Response times can differ based on day, time, and access conditions.
- Quote structures vary by provider and job scope.
- Follow-up availability should be confirmed before booking.

## Practical comparison checklist
1. Request a written scope and exclusions.
2. Ask for a full pricing breakdown before work starts.
3. Confirm estimated timeline and follow-up process.
4. Ask which details may trigger additional charges.
5. Keep quotes and invoices for comparison.

## Before confirming a provider
- Ask for the latest contact details directly.
- Confirm service availability for your locality.
- Confirm pricing and schedule in writing.

Please confirm information directly with the provider before making a booking decision.
